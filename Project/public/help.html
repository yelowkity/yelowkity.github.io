We are here to help

Our community centers search page (Home Page) uses search function to help users locate the community center by name, part of the name within the Prince George community, and its distance to the location of the user.

Once you load the page in the browser, think of name of the desired community center by name and type it in the search window. if the system has the name or community center in the database you will see the list of the centers prompted once the search button is called. Also, the search sunction will provide the distance from the user to the desired center location. Please make sure you agree to disclose and enter "agree" your current location when prompted by the search engine. You will see the small window with 'This file wants to' script telling 'Know your location' and presenting 'Allow' and 'Block' buttons Please choose 'Allow to let our function file to search the distance from your location to the desired Community Center in search.
